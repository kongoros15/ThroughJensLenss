About Jen

Hello! I’m Jen — an artist exploring the quiet intersection of shape, texture, and memory. My work spans illustration, photography, and digital painting. I love translating your stories into custom artwork that feels personal and lasting.

When I’m not sketching, you’ll find me chasing soft light and playful color palettes that feel like a deep breath.
